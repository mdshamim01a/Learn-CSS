Winter is one of the most pleasant and beautiful seasons of the year. It usually begins in November and lasts till February. The weather becomes cool and refreshing, and people enjoy wearing warm clothes. The mornings are foggy, and the nights are long and quiet.
In many places, people sit around bonfires to keep themselves warm. Hot food and drinks like tea, soup, and coffee taste especially good during winter. It is also the time when many fruits and vegetables are available. Winter is a season of comfort, freshness, and celebration.
